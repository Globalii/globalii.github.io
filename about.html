About Us

Every great journey starts with a single step, and at Global Interpreting Innovations (GII), we are here to guide interpreters on that path. Our story began in the heart of Minnesota, where a passionate, women-owned, minority-led team saw the growing need for professional interpreters in communities, schools, hospitals, and social services. We believed in building bridges between cultures and languages, ensuring no voice goes unheard. Today, we stand as a beacon for those ready to make a difference through interpretation.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Expert Teachers: Picture learning from trainers who’ve walked the path before you. Their real-life stories and expertise make every session engaging and relatable.
  • Complete Curriculum: Think of our program as a map. It guides you through essential topics like ethics, decision-making, and cultural sensitivity, ensuring you’re ready for any interpreting challenge.
  • Interactive Learning: Imagine role plays, practical exercises, and hands-on activities that turn learning into an adventure.
  • Accessible Learning: With all classes held on Zoom, you can join from anywhere, making your dreams more attainable than ever.
  • Affordable Options: We understand budgets, so our training is priced lower than most competitors, giving you unmatched value.

Our Flagship Program: The Community Interpreter® Training

Imagine a program that transforms eager learners into confident professionals. That’s the promise of The Community Interpreter® International training, a trusted 40-hour certificate program. At GII, this flagship course is more than just lessons; it’s a dynamic experience where interpreters gain the skills and tools they need to shine. Through interactive sessions, real-world scenarios, and cutting-edge resources, we prepare interpreters to tackle the challenges of medical, educational, and community interpreting with confidence and professionalism.
