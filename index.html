Welcome to Global Interpreting Innovations

At Global Interpreting Innovations (GII), we believe that effective communication is the key to building bridges and empowering communities. As a women-owned business based in Minnesota, we are proud to bring exceptional training and professional development opportunities to interpreters nationwide and beyond.

Transforming Lives Through Language

Interpreters play a critical role in connecting individuals across cultural and linguistic divides. At GII, we are committed to equipping interpreters with the skills, knowledge, and resources they need to excel in their careers. Our flagship program, The Community Interpreter® International, is the most trusted and comprehensive 40-hour certificate training program for interpreters. This program is designed to:

  • Prepare interpreters for work in medical, educational, and social service settings.
  • Provide a solid foundation in interpreting ethics, skills, and protocols.
  • Empower participants with the tools needed to navigate real-world challenges.

Why Choose GII?

  • Women-Owned and Operated: As a proud women-owned business, we are dedicated to fostering inclusivity and empowering voices across communities.
  • Minnesota Roots, Global Reach: While we are based in Minnesota, our programs serve interpreters across the nation and internationally.
  • Expert-Led Training: Our certified trainers bring extensive experience and real-world knowledge to every session.
  • Interactive and Engaging Learning: Hands-on activities, role plays, and practical exercises make our training effective and enjoyable.

Setting the Standard in Interpreter Training

When you choose Global Interpreting Innovations, you’re not just enrolling in a course—you’re joining a movement dedicated to advancing language access and communication equity. Our training programs are built on the principles of excellence, innovation, and accessibility, ensuring that every interpreter leaves prepared to make a meaningful impact.
